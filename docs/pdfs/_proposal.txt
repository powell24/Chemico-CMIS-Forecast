5  Most Recommended

If Chemico can only start with two, lead with these. Together they create the fastest, most defensible path from AI investment to
measurable EBITDA improvement.

01 Demand Forecasting & Inventory AI

This is the fastest-payback use case. Chemico manages chemicals across 50 sites -- even a modest improvement in forecast
accuracy frees up working capital and eliminates costly stockouts. CFOs and ops leaders immediately understand it because it
ties directly to cash flow. It's also the easiest to demo with existing ERP data.

Reduced working capital À Fewer stockouts À Lower expedited freight

02 Compliance & SDS Intelligence Copilot

Compliance exposure is a direct valuation risk -- especially across the regulated industries Chemico serves (aerospace,
biopharma, government). An SDS copilot saves hours of manual review per week and documents compliance at every site. If
Leon is thinking about any future equity event, this de-risks the business in a way that buyers and boards immediately value.

Lower compliance labor À Reduced noncompliance risk À Valuation protection

DEMO STRATEGY

Show demand/inventory AI first -- it's tangible and ties to cash flow. Then show the compliance copilot as the risk-reduction layer.
Together they tell a single story: better operations, lower risk, higher valuation.
